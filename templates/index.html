If you don’t already know, global temperatures have been rising at an alarming rate. We look on with horror as our climate changes right before our eyes.

A major contributor to climate change is greenhouse gases produced from large equipment like factories, including carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide.

There are several ways that we can stop climate change in its tracks. These include protecting and restoring key ecosystems, supporting small agricultural producers, promoting green energy,combatting short-lived climant pollutants, and working on reducing emmissions from items such as cars.
